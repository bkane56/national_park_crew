## 3. Distance and Travel Between Parks  

- **Salt Lake City to Moab** (Arches & Canyonlands): ~230 miles, approx. 4-hour drive  
- **Moab to Torrey** (Capitol Reef): ~120 miles, approx. 2.5-hour drive  
- **Torrey to Bryce Canyon City** (Bryce Canyon): ~115 miles, approx. 2.5-hour drive

Distances and driving times support a workably paced itinerary with travel breaks and sightseeing en route.

## 4. Detailed Daily Itinerary  

### Day 1: July 18 – Arrival and Arches National Park  
- 12:00 PM: Arrive Salt Lake City International Airport  
- Pick up rental car  
- Drive to Moab (~4 hours)  
- Check-in at Red Cliffs Lodge  
- Short evening scenic drive in Arches National Park vicinity  
- Sunset view at Park Avenue viewpoint (~1 hour)  
- Overnight in Moab  

### Day 2: July 19 – Full Day at Arches National Park  
- Morning hike: Devil’s Garden Trail (4-5 hours), moderate hiking featuring Landscape Arch and Double O Arch  
- Lunch in Moab  
- Afternoon visit: Windows Section and Balanced Rock for shorter, easy hikes (~2 hours)  
- Optional evening stargazing at Arches Visitor Center area  
- Overnight in Moab at Hoodoo Moab Hotel  

### Day 3: July 20 – Canyonlands National Park and Travel to Torrey  
- Morning: Explore Canyonlands Island in the Sky District:  
  - Mesa Arch viewpoint (30 minutes)  
  - Grand View Point Trail (2 hours)  
  - Green River Overlook and other scenic stops  
- Afternoon: Drive to Torrey (approx. 2.5 hours)  
- Evening: Check in at Capitol Reef Resort, rest and dinner  

### Day 4: July 21 – Capitol Reef National Park  
- Morning: Scenic drive on Capitol Reef Scenic Drive  
- Hike to Hickman Bridge (2-3 hours)  
- Lunch in Fruita Historic District  
- Afternoon: Petroglyph viewing, orchards, optional Cassidy Arch hike (3 hours)  
- Evening: Relax at lodge  

### Day 5: July 22 – Travel and Bryce Canyon National Park  
- Morning: Drive to Bryce Canyon City (approx. 2.5 hours)  
- Afternoon: Visitor Center and Rim Trail hikes to Sunset and Sunrise Points (2-3 hours)  
- Evening: Stay at Best Western Plus Bryce Canyon Grand Hotel  

### Day 6: July 23 – Explore Bryce Canyon National Park  
- Morning: Queen’s Garden/Navajo Loop hike (3-4 hours)  
- Lunch  
- Afternoon: Scenic drives to Bryce Point, Inspiration Point, Fairyland Point (optional)  
- Evening: Stargazing (Bryce Canyon known for dark skies)  

### Day 7: July 24 – Travel to Zion National Park  
- Morning: Drive to Springdale (~1.5 hours)  
- Early afternoon check-in at Bumbleberry Inn  
- Visit Zion Canyon Visitor Center  
- Shuttle to Emerald Pools Trail (2-3 hours)  
- Evening: Relax and explore Springdale  

### Day 8: July 25 – Zion National Park Full Day  
- Morning: Angels Landing hike (5-6 hours, strenuous, permit required) or Observation Point hike (similar duration)  
- Afternoon: Riverside Walk or Lower Narrows hike (2-4 hours, easy to moderate, water shoes recommended if entering water)  
- Evening: Rest and dinner in Springdale  

### Day 9: July 26 – Zion National Park  
- Morning: Kolob Canyons or Watchman Trail exploration (2-3 hours)  
- Afternoon: Zion Human History Museum and short hikes  
- Evening: Final dinner in Springdale  

### Day 10: July 27 – Departure Day  
- Morning: Leisurely breakfast, check out  
- Drive to Salt Lake City International Airport (~4.5 hours)  
- Return rental car  
- Flight departs at 1:00 PM from SLC  
- Arrive SRQ at 7:00 PM

## 5. Additional Notes and Recommendations  

- **Permits:** Angels Landing hiking permit required; apply early well before trip.  
- **Packing:** Prepare for warm, dry weather; include sun protection, sturdy hiking shoes, hydration systems, and layers for cooler evenings.  
- **Transportation:** Rental car essential for travel between parks; consider fuel stops in Moab, Torrey, and Springdale.  
- **Meals:** Use local grocery stores for breakfasts or picnic supplies to save costs.  
- **Flexibility:** The itinerary allows for adjustments based on hikers’ pace and weather conditions.  
- **Stargazing:** Take advantage of dark skies in Bryce Canyon and Arches for night sky viewing.  
- **Booking:** Reserve accommodations and flights as early as possible for best pricing and availability.
