## 4. Detailed Daily Itinerary

### Day 1 – May 15, 2026: Arrival and Guadalupe Mountains Exploration  
- **Arrival:** 12:30 PM at El Paso International Airport (ELP) via American Airlines (AA 1453)  
- **Transport:** Drive ~2 hours to Guadalupe Mountains National Park.  
- **Check-in:** Guadalupe Mountains Inn in Salt Flat or Pine Springs Campground inside the park (depending on preference).  
- **Activities:**  
  - Visitor Center orientation (about 1 hour).  
  - Light afternoon hike: Start Guadalupe Peak Trail or McKittrick Canyon Rim Trail (2-3 hours).  
  - Evening stargazing session near Pine Springs (approx. 45 minutes).  
- **Pacing:** Easy first day to adjust after travel.

---

### Day 2 – May 16, 2026: Full Hiking Day at Guadalupe Mountains NP  
- **Morning:** Guadalupe Peak Trail (8.4 miles roundtrip) hike to Texas' highest peak (7-8 hours).  
- **Afternoon:** Scenic drive and hike to Lower Falls on McKittrick Canyon Trail (2-3 hours).  
- **Evening:** Relax or optional short walk to Smith Spring for bird watching (1 hour).  
- **Pacing:** Active day with strenuous hiking and scenic exploration.

---

### Day 3 – May 17, 2026: Canyons and Wildlife at Guadalupe Mountains NP  
- **Morning:** Devil’s Hall Trail (7.4 mi roundtrip; 3-4 hours) for canyon and rock formations.  
- **Early Afternoon:** Short hikes to viewpoints such as Guadalupe Canyon Overlook or Permian Reef Trail (2-3 hours).  
- **Evening:** Attend ranger programs or enjoy self-guided bird watching/photography.  
- **Pacing:** Moderate hiking and nature observation.

---

### Day 4 – May 18, 2026: Travel to Carlsbad Caverns National Park & Initial Exploration  
- **Morning:** Drive ~1.5 hours to Carlsbad Caverns National Park.  
- **Midday:** Visitor Center orientation and preparation for cave tours.  
- **Afternoon:** Self-Guided Big Room Cave tour (1.5-2 hours).  
- **Evening:** Attend the famous Bat Flight Program at sunset (about 1 hour).  
- **Overnight:** Stay at Comfort Suites Carlsbad or La Quinta Inn & Suites in Carlsbad.  
- **Pacing:** Mixture of underground exploration and evening wildlife spectacle.

---

### Day 5 – May 19, 2026: Additional Cave and Surface Activities in Carlsbad area  
- **Morning:** Explore other caves such as King’s Palace or Spider Cave via guided tours (2-3 hours).  
- **Afternoon:** Hike desert trails near visitor center (1-2 hours). Optional visit to Living Desert Zoo and Gardens State Park (about 2 hours).  
- **Evening:** Free time to relax or explore local dining.  
- **Pacing:** Balanced day of cavern exploration and surface ecology.

---

### Day 6 – May 20, 2026: Travel to White Sands National Park & Initial Exploration  
- **Morning:** Drive approx. 2.5 hours from Carlsbad to White Sands NP.  
- **Midday:** Visitor Center orientation (30 minutes).  
- **Afternoon:** Explore Dune Life Nature Trail and Alkali Flat Trail (1-3 hours).  
- **Fun Activity:** Sand sledding on dunes (1-2 hours).  
- **Evening:** Sunset photography and relaxation among gypsum dunes (1 hour).  
- **Overnight:** La Quinta Inn & Suites or Days Inn in Alamogordo.  
- **Pacing:** Outdoor adventure and natural wonders appreciation.

---

### Day 7 – May 21, 2026: Final Morning at White Sands & Departure  
- **Early Morning:** Optional short hike or sunrise photography session (1-2 hours).  
- **Late Morning:** Drive ~3 hours to Albuquerque International Sunport (ABQ) for departure.  
- **Flight:** Depart ABQ to Miami via Southwest Airlines (WN 649, 11 AM flight) or Delta Air Lines (DL 3289, 3 PM flight on May 21).  
- **Pacing:** Relaxed morning to reflect on trip and prepare for journey home.

## 7. Recommendations & Tips

- **Booking:** Reserve flights and accommodations early for best rates and availability.  
- **Transportation:** Rent a reliable car upon arrival at El Paso; essential for accessing parks and lodgings.  
- **Packing:** Bring hiking gear, sun protection, layered clothing (desert nights are cool), and camera for scenic photography.  
- **Park Preparation:** Check each park’s official website for alerts, trail closures, and ranger programs.  
- **Health & Safety:** Carry sufficient water, especially for long hikes, and inform someone of your hiking plans.  
- **Flexibility:** Use the flexible return flight date to allow an extra day at your preferred park or rest day.
