## 4. Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: May 13 — Arrival & Yosemite National Park  
- Arrival at Fresno Yosemite International Airport by 12:30 PM.  
- Pick up rental car and drive ~1.5 hours to Yosemite View Lodge, El Portal — check-in.  
- Afternoon visit: Yosemite Valley Visitor Center, Yosemite Falls viewpoint (easy 1-2 hour stroll).  
- Dinner and overnight at Yosemite View Lodge.

### Day 2: May 14 — Yosemite National Park (Waterfalls & Meadow)  
- Morning walk: Mirror Lake Trail (~2 miles roundtrip, 1.5-2 hours) and Lower Yosemite Falls Trail (easy, 1 hour).  
- Picnic lunch in Yosemite Valley.  
- Afternoon sightseeing: Bridalveil Fall viewpoint and Tunnel View (iconic vistas, easy walk).  
- Optional evening ranger program participation.  
- Overnight at Yosemite View Lodge.

### Day 3: May 15 — Yosemite National Park (Panoramic Views & Giant Sequoias)  
- Morning drive to Glacier Point (~1 hour), enjoy panoramic views including Half Dome (~2 hours).  
- Afternoon visit to Mariposa Grove of Giant Sequoias, moderate 1-2 hour hike.  
- Return to Yosemite View Lodge for dinner and rest.

### Day 4: May 16 — Yosemite National Park (Optional Intensive Hiking)  
- Option 1: Hike Mist Trail to Vernal and Nevada Falls (6.5 miles roundtrip, strenuous 5-6 hours).  
- Option 2: If Half Dome permit secured, hike Half Dome (full-day strenuous hike, permit required).  
- Evening relaxation at Yosemite View Lodge.

### Day 5: May 17 — Travel to Sequoia National Park  
- Check out from Yosemite View Lodge.  
- Drive approx. 2.5 hours to Wuksachi Lodge, Sequoia National Park.  
- Afternoon visit to General Sherman Tree and easy hike on Big Trees Trail (1-2 hours).  
- Optional evening ranger program.  
- Overnight at Wuksachi Lodge.

### Day 6: May 18 — Sequoia National Park Highlights  
- Morning hike: Moro Rock Trail (0.5 miles, steep, 30-45 minutes).  
- Midday: Crescent Meadow Loop Trail (1.8 miles, ~1 hour).  
- Afternoon visit: Tunnel Log and relaxation at lodge. Optional horseback riding.  
- Overnight at Wuksachi Lodge.

### Day 7: May 19 — Sequoia to Kings Canyon National Park  
- Morning check-out, drive ~1 hour to John Muir Lodge, Grant Grove Village.  
- Afternoon visit General Grant Tree (easy short walk) and Grant Grove Visitor Center.  
- Hike Zumwalt Meadow Loop Trail (2.9 miles, 2 hours, easy-moderate).  
- Overnight at John Muir Lodge.

### Day 8: May 20 — Departure Day  
- Morning check-out.  
- Drive ~2.5–3 hours to Fresno Yosemite International Airport.  
- Return rental car.  
- Depart FAT at 1:30 PM on American Airlines flight AA 5678 to Orlando, arriving 8:00 PM.

## 6. Additional Trip Notes

- **Rental Car:** Recommended for airport transfer and park travel due to distances and convenience.  
- **Park Entrance Fees:** National Park Passes may be purchased in advance or upon entry for each park.  
- **Packing:** Prepare layers for moderate spring weather, hiking shoes, sun protection, and water.  
- **Permit Considerations:** Half Dome hike requires permit, apply early if interested.  
- **Ranger Programs:** Most evenings offer ranger talks or guided walks, good to enhance experience.  
- **Health & Safety:** Moderate hiking included; travelers should assess personal fitness for optional strenuous hikes.
