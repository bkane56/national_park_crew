# Western Utah National Parks Itinerary Report  
---

## Overview  
This detailed itinerary outlines a 16-day winter visit to premier National Parks located within 8 hours of Salt Lake City International Airport (SLC), Utah, with a final departure from St. George Regional Airport (SGU). The trip is designed for optimal scenic and hiking experiences in Arches, Canyonlands, Bryce Canyon, and Zion National Parks. Accommodations have been chosen for convenience and comfort near each park, with travel logistics and flight recommendations provided.

---

## Flights and Transportation  

### Departure Flight  
- **Departure Airport:** Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport (SRQ), near Venice, Florida  
- **Arrival Airport:** Salt Lake City International Airport (SLC), Utah  
- **Departure Date:** 2025-12-20  
- **Note:** Venice Municipal Airport (VNC) does not offer direct commercial flights to Salt Lake City; Sarasota-Bradenton (SRQ) is the recommended departure airport for best flight availability. Use flight search engines (Kayak, Expedia, Google Flights) to book suitable flights.

### Arrival and Ground Transport  
- Upon arrival at Salt Lake City International Airport, rent a vehicle for the multi-park itinerary. Driving times range from 1 to 4 hours between parks and accommodation sites.

### Return Flight  
- **Departure Airport:** St. George Regional Airport (SGU), Utah  
- **Arrival Airport:** Sarasota-Bradenton International Airport (SRQ), Florida  
- **Return Date:** 2026-01-05  
- **Note:** Fly back from SGU as it provides convenience when concluding park visits around Zion, reducing overall transit time. As with the outbound flight, check multiple flight portals to book efficient and budget-friendly flights.

## Accommodations Summary  

### Near Arches National Park (Moab area)  
- Options: Hotels, motels, inns available in Moab town  
- Criteria: Proximity (5-15 min drive), amenities, winter warmth  
- Estimated Nightly Cost Range: $120-$200

### Near Canyonlands National Park (Needles District)  
- Lodging near Needles district is limited, consider camping or lodging in Monticello or Needles vicinity (~30 min drive)  
- Estimated Nightly Cost Range: $80-$150, with some rustic options

### Near Bryce Canyon National Park  
- Bryce Canyon Lodge or nearby Tropic/ Bryce Canyon City hotels  
- Amenities: Heating and winter-appropriate facilities recommended  
- Estimated Nightly Cost Range: $130-$250

### Near Zion National Park (Springdale)  
- Variety of hotels and lodges located within 5-10 minutes of park entrance  
- Estimated Nightly Cost Range: $150-$300 depending on quality and season

## Notes and Recommendations  

- **Flight Booking:** Due to lack of direct flights at Venice Municipal Airport (VNC), departing from SRQ and returning via SGU creates time and logistical advantages. Be sure to book early for winter holiday season.  
- **Vehicle Rental:** Reserve a 4WD or all-wheel-drive vehicle due to winter conditions and varied terrain.  
- **Weather:** Expect cold weather and possible snow; pack accordingly and monitor park alerts. Certain hikes (like Narrows or Angels Landing) may require permits and have seasonal restrictions.  
- **Park Permits:** Obtain necessary permits in advance (Angels Landing hike, possible full moon hikes).  
- **Park Passes:** Purchase America the Beautiful annual park pass for savings across multiple parks.  
- **Health and Safety:** Bring water, layers, hiking gear, and emergency kits during hikes.
