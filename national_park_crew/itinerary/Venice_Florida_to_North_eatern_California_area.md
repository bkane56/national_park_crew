## Detailed Day-by-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 – May 13: Arrival and Yosemite National Park  
- Arrival at Fresno Yosemite International Airport at ~1:30 PM  
- Drive to Yosemite Valley (Approx. 1.5 hours)  
- Check-in at **Yosemite Valley Lodge** for 3 nights  
- Afternoon: Take a scenic orientation walk near Yosemite Falls  
- Evening: Early dinner at lodge and optional stargazing session in the valley  

---

### Day 2 – May 14: Yosemite Valley Waterfalls & Hiking  
- Morning hike to Yosemite Falls (3-4 hours)  
- Picnic lunch in Yosemite Valley meadow  
- Afternoon visit to Mirror Lake (2 hours) or bike rental with casual valley floor tour (2-3 hours)  
- Evening: Attend a ranger-led interpretive program (~1 hour)  

---

### Day 3 – May 15: Glacier Point and Giant Sequoias  
- Morning drive or shuttle to Glacier Point; panoramic views and exploration (2 hours)  
- Midday visit to Mariposa Grove of Giant Sequoias; hike among sequoias (2-3 hours)  
- Return to Yosemite Valley in the afternoon  
- Evening: Relax and dine at Yosemite Valley Lodge  

---

### Day 4 – May 16: Tioga Road and Tuolumne Meadows  
- Early morning drive to Tuolumne Meadows via Tioga Road (approx. 1 hour from valley)  
  *Note: Tioga Road typically opens late May or June; confirm before trip*  
- Moderate hike: Soda Springs and Parsons Lodge or Gaylor Lakes (2-3 hours)  
- Picnic lunch in meadow  
- Late afternoon return to Yosemite Valley or transfer to **Evergreen Lodge** at Groveland for 2 nights stay  
- Evening rest  

---

### Day 5 – May 17: Half Dome Viewing and Travel to Sequoia/Kings Canyon  
- Morning optional activities: rock climbing intro or horseback riding (2-3 hours)  
- Free time for last Yosemite visits or pack and prepare for departure  
- Afternoon drive to Sequoia/Kings Canyon area (~3.5 - 4 hours drive)  
- Check into **Montecito Sequoia Lodge** for 2 nights  

---

### Day 6 – May 18: Sequoia National Park – Giant Forest & Moro Rock  
- Morning visit to General Sherman Tree and hiking in Giant Forest (3 hours)  
- Midday hike up Moro Rock, including climb and summit views (1-2 hours)  
- Afternoon free for relaxing or short nature walks nearby  
- Evening: Optional ranger program or stargazing opportunity  

---

### Day 7 – May 19: Kings Canyon National Park Exploration  
- Morning visit to Grant Grove, see General Grant Tree, visitor center (1-2 hours)  
- Midday scenic drive along Kings Canyon Scenic Byway to Cedar Grove with stops  
- Afternoon hike Zumwalt Meadow Trail or riverwalk (1-2 hours)  
- Evening check-in at **Comfort Inn & Suites** in Three Rivers, CA (1 night)  

---

### Day 8 – May 20: Departure Day  
- Morning: Leisurely breakfast and last nature walks near Three Rivers area  
- Drive to Fresno Yosemite International Airport (~1.5 hours)  
- Afternoon departure flight (2:30 PM to 4:00 PM) via American, Delta or United Airlines

## Notes & Recommendations

- Early reservations for Yosemite Valley Lodge and popular lodges are critical, as May is a popular season.

- Flights from Tampa (TPA) have the best balance of travel time and cost; Sarasota (SRQ) requires two stops; Orlando (MCO) is also viable.  
- Car rental at Fresno Airport is recommended for flexibility to reach and explore parks.  
- Permit or ranger-led activities may require advance booking.  
- National Parks pass bought upon arrival will cover all three parks for the week.
